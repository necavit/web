Information collection on this site

No personal information is collected, stored or analyzed directly by this site. The only information being collected is through third-party services (Google Analytics and Disqus), which, in turn, have their own privacy policies.

You can disable the use of those services by rejecting this site's policy.

This site might use cookies

This site will only use cookies if you accept the following privacy policy. If you reject this policy, the site will still provide its content, but some features will be disabled.

Read the information below to know what cookies are, why does this site need them and what can you do to avoid them being placed on your device.

Finally, you can either accept or reject the privacy policy by clicking on the corresponding buttons at the end of this page.

Cookies are...

... small pieces of text that this site and third parties install as files in your browser. These files are sent back to the originating site, which recognises them and uses them to remember your preferences, for example, or to enable advanced services or additional features.

Following the EU Commission regulation concerning cookies, users must be informed and consent must be given to use and install certain types of cookies (such as persistent and third-party ones).

Cookies can be classified depending on their:

Persistence

Session cookies are erased when the user closes the browser.

Persistent cookies remain on the user's computer/device for a pre-defined period of time.

Domain

First-party cookies are set by the web server of the visited page and share the same domain.

Third-party cookies are stored by a different domain to the visited page's domain.
